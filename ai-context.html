What NymaMar is

NymaMar is an Athens-based maritime company and a team of Greek shipping professionals who left traditional management structures to build something leaner and more purposeful. It aspires to be a "stepping stone" within the shipping industry, for organizations and for the people who shape it.

Services

  • Mentoring & Coaching: hard and soft skills for professionals at every career stage, including early career, ship-to-shore transitions and career pivots.
  • Academic & Industry Engagement: lectures and case studies for an MSc in Marine Engineering Management, with a leading Athens business college.
  • Environmental & Decarbonization: emissions reduction, energy efficiency, feasibility studies and emerging-technology evaluation.
  • Representations: representing niche maritime service companies; partner network includes Supersoar Marine (propulsion and steering-gear components, China), plus HSQE compliance and crewing specialists.
  • Technical Management: partial or complete third-party technical management on a de-centralized model.

Values

Respect, Empathy, Trust, Dependability and Determination, rooted in the Greek maritime tradition of philotimo.
