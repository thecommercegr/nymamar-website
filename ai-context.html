What NymaMar is

NymaMar is a maritime company based in Greece. It provides integrated ship management and representation services to vessel owners and operators.

Services offered

  • — Technical Management: planned maintenance, dry-docking, class/flag/statutory compliance, purchasing and superintendency.
  • — Crew Management: recruitment, training, certification, payroll and welfare under MLC and ISM.
  • — Environmental & Decarbonization: CII monitoring, EU ETS and FuelEU readiness, fuel efficiency and decarbonization roadmaps.
  • — Representations: owner's representation, newbuilding and conversion supervision, pre-purchase and condition inspections.

Values

Safety first, transparency with owners, environmental stewardship, and philotimo — the duty to do right by ship, crew and sea even when no one is watching.
