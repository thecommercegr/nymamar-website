Who we are

A steady hand on the world's oldest trade.

Our story

Founded by mariners, run for owners.

NymaMar was created by people who have stood on the bridge and in the engine room — and who believe ship management should feel like a partnership, not a transaction.

From our home in Greece, a nation that has carried the world's cargo for millennia, we manage vessels with the discipline of class rules and the conscience of seafarers. Every decision is weighed against three questions: is it safe, is it right, and would we accept it for our own ship?
