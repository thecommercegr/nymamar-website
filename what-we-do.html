What we do

The whole of ship management, handled with care.

NymaMar offers integrated management across the life of a vessel. Owners can hand us the full scope or select the disciplines they need — the standard never changes.

01

Technical Management

We keep vessels safe, certified and commercially available — from planned maintenance and spares to dry-docking, class surveys and 24/7 superintendency.

  • — Planned maintenance & condition monitoring
  • — Dry-dock planning & supervision
  • — Class, flag & statutory compliance
  • — Purchasing, spares & logistics
02

Crew Management

Competent, well-treated seafarers are the heart of a safe ship. We recruit, train, certify and care for crews who are proud to sail under our management.

  • — Recruitment & manning
  • — Training, competence & appraisal
  • — Payroll, welfare & retention
  • — MLC & ISM compliance
03

Environmental & Decarbonization

We help owners meet today's rules and tomorrow's targets — turning regulatory pressure into measurable efficiency and a credible path to lower emissions.

  • — CII rating & performance monitoring
  • — EU ETS & FuelEU readiness
  • — Fuel efficiency & voyage optimisation
  • — Decarbonization roadmaps
04

Representations

As your representative, we are your trusted presence — supervising newbuildings, attending vessels and protecting your interests wherever your ships trade.

  • — Owner's representation
  • — Newbuilding & conversion supervision
  • — Pre-purchase & condition inspections
  • — Local liaison & agency oversight
