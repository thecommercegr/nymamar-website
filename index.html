Who we are

A maritime company built on quiet, relentless diligence.

From our base in Greece, NymaMar brings together seasoned shipmanagers, engineers and operators who treat every ship under our flag as our own — protecting the asset, the crew and the environment in equal measure.

We keep our fleet small enough to know each vessel by name and large enough to deliver world-class technical, crewing and compliance standards.
